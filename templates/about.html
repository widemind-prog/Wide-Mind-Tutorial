About Wide Mind Tutorials

Wide Mind Tutorials is an academic learning platform created to make quality university education clearer, easier, and more accessible for students.

Our Story

Wide Mind Tutorials began unofficially in 2022 as a small academic support initiative for students seeking clearer explanations beyond the lecture hall.

Due to consistency, results, and student feedback, the tutorial was formally launched in 2024. In 2026, it evolved into a fully integrated online learning platform to improve access, structure, and flexibility.

Our Focus

At Wide Mind Tutorials, we believe that understanding is the foundation of academic success. Our focus is on simplifying complex academic concepts and presenting official school materials in a way students can truly understand and apply.

  • Clear and simplified academic explanations
  • Well-structured, summarized notes
  • Recorded explanations for flexible learning
  • Focused preparation for tests and examinations

Who We Serve

Wide Mind Tutorials currently serves UI DLC Psychology students. All materials are carefully aligned with official course outlines to ensure relevance, accuracy, and academic integrity.

The Tutor

Wide Mind Tutorials is facilitated by Mr. Oluwasegun OREOTAN Samuel, an Educational Psychologist, an Educator, and a digitally inclined academic mentor.

“Understanding is key to academic success.”

His teaching approach focuses on transforming official school materials into easy-to-understand summaries, supported by clear and structured recorded explanations.

Student Experiences

Over the years, students have reported improved understanding, stronger confidence, and better academic performance through Wide Mind Tutorials.
