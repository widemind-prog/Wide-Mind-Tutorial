Privacy Policy

At Wide Mind Tutorials, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide our tutorial services effectively. This may include:

  • Your name and basic contact details
  • Account and login information
  • Payment confirmation details
  • Academic-related information needed for tutorials

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide access to tutorial materials and recordings
  • Manage student accounts and subscriptions
  • Communicate important updates and announcements
  • Improve the quality of our learning services

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Information may only be shared when required to process payments or when legally required.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. While no online system is completely secure, appropriate safeguards are maintained.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request account deletion where applicable

Terms & Conditions

Account Ownership

Each account is strictly personal to the registered student. Sharing login details or tutorial materials with others is not permitted.

Accounts found engaging in unauthorized sharing may be suspended or terminated.

Payments & Access

All payments grant access to tutorial materials for the specified academic period (e.g., a semester).

Access is only activated after successful payment confirmation. Prices may change, but confirmed payments will always honor the price paid at the time.

Suspension of Accounts

Wide Mind Tutorials reserves the right to suspend or restrict accounts if:

  • There is misuse of the platform
  • Unauthorized sharing of content is detected
  • False or misleading account information is provided

Account Deletion

Students may request account deletion at any time. Once deleted, access to materials will be permanently removed.

Deleted accounts cannot be recovered.

Refund Policy

Due to the digital nature of our tutorial materials, payments are generally non-refundable once access has been granted.

Refunds may only be considered in exceptional cases, such as verified payment errors or duplicate charges.

Policy Updates

These policies may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in services or operational needs. Updated versions will always be published on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or Terms & Conditions, please contact us through the official communication channels provided in the footer.
